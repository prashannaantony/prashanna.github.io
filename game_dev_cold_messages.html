What works vs what to avoid
Do
Mention a specific game they made
State you're available immediately
Keep it under 150 words on LinkedIn
Include a direct portfolio link
Send to recruiter AND a team lead
Follow up once after 5–7 days
Avoid
"I am very passionate about games"
Sending the same text to everyone
Attaching CV without being asked
Messaging on weekends
Sending 3+ follow-ups
Spelling mistakes (use Grammarly)
